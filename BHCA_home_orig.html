Comedy Night 2014 is Sold Out!

Thanks to everyone for your tremendous support – If you would like to be put on a waiting list please email Carlyn Paynter . We will contact you directly the week of May 12th should space become available.

Saturday, May 17, 2014 at 6:30pm – Rowayton Community Center

Join us again under the stars at the Community Center for Comedy Night 2014. Craig Spinney, a Rowayton resident and stage manager for Jon Stewart’s “The Daily Show” on Comedy Central, will once again bring us big name comedians that will surely have you laughing! In addition to being a great time for all, Comedy Night is one of the largest fundraisers for the Rowayton Civic Association which provides funding for the additional events throughout the year.

The evening will include first-rate entertainment, seating at a table, beer and bottled water (BYO picnic and other spirits).

Last year’s event was the largest and most successful ever and sold out in record time. So gather your friends, pack a picnic and join the fun while supporting the RCA at the same time!

Note: there are a limited number of tables for 12, and they will be located around the perimeter of the tent.

For more information, contact Jennifer Friedrichsen or Carlyn Paynter.

This event is for ages 21 and over.

About the RCA

The mission of the Rowayton Civic Association is to enrich the lives of Rowayton residents and neighbors through providing cultural programs, educational events and supporting local community initiatives. The RCA invites all residents to become involved in the RCA through volunteerism and helping to identify the needs of our community.
